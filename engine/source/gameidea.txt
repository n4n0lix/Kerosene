Story Ideas:

1. Poor Familiy, Daughter died of young age, father went to necromancer to ressurect daughter for exchange of his life
	(blood trade because they have no money to pay priest). Father dies, daughter ressurected. Daughter doesn't understand

	nothing to do with her, daughter is even more confused and blames herself for all the miserie. She wants to be dead again
	because she thinks this will fix the problems, but what ever she tries, she just can't die anymore.
